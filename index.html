I'm a PhD student in the CS program at University of Utah. I am advised by Dr. Daniel Brown . I graduated with MS in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University. And what seems like an alternate universe now , I attended Panjab University for Electronics and Communication Engineering

My research aims to develop principled reinforcement learning (RL) algorithms that obtain state-of-the-art performance with a higher degree of simplicity, scalability, and robustness than current method. Much of my work uses ideas for probabilistic inference to makes progress on a number of problems in RL, such as long horizon reasoning, representation learning , and exploration.

Research opportunities: I am usually looking for students to help with research projects both during the semester and over the summer. If you are interested, please send me an email. I especially encourage students from underrepresented groups to reach out.
